Campus Screening Clinics - Confidential HIV Testing for Students

₹ 499 ₹ 1500
Results Available in 2-3 Weeks

Campus Screening Clinics are designed to offer students easy access to confidential HIV testing and health screenings. Our clinics are located on or near campuses, providing a convenient and safe environment to get tested. We aim to support student health and wellness, offering timely, reliable testing in a non-judgmental space.

Whether you’re managing your sexual health or getting tested for the first time, our friendly healthcare team is here to support you throughout the process, ensuring a seamless experience from start to finish.

-*Note:-
* Consultation services available for further health guidance and support.




Service Details



Window Period:

Get reliable results within 2-3 weeks after potential HIV exposure.

Sample Required:

A small blood sample is all that's required for HIV testing.

Special Preparation:

No fasting or special preparation required before testing.

Reports:

Results are instantly available on-site or can be securely delivered to your email.

Home Sample Collection:

Home collection services are available upon request for added convenience.

Tele-consult with Doctor:

Speak with a doctor online to discuss your results and receive personalized health advice.

What Does the Test Include?

- HIV p24 antigen for early detection
- HIV antibodies for HIV-1 and HIV-2
- Comprehensive HIV screening for overall health monitoring

Where Can I Get Tested?

Visit any of our Campus Screening Clinics on participating campuses or request home sample collection services.

When Should I Test?

Testing is recommended 2-3 weeks after potential HIV exposure. Routine testing is encouraged for sexually active students or those at risk.
