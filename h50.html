The Wellness Testing & Support package is designed to help individuals monitor and maintain their overall health with a focus on preventive care. Whether you're aiming to stay on top of your general health or address specific wellness concerns, this comprehensive package provides a range of tests and support services to keep you feeling your best.

  • Comprehensive Health Screening: This package includes a wide range of health tests, such as blood pressure checks, cholesterol, blood sugar levels, liver and kidney function tests, and more. These tests are designed managing your wellness.

-*Note:-
* All tests and counseling are confidential, and services are personalized to suit your specific health needs.




Service Details



Purpose:

Providing comprehensive wellness testing and ongoing support for individuals living with HIV or at risk, focusing on maintaining overall health and well-being.

Services Offered:

HIV testing, health screenings, mental wellness assessments, nutritional counseling, fitness guidance, and regular follow-ups to monitor and maintain optimal health.

Special Preparation:

No special preparation needed. Bring your medical history and any current health concerns to discuss with the wellness advisor.

Cost:

Affordable pricing with bundled packages for comprehensive wellness testing and ongoing support. Some services may be covered by insurance.

Location:

Available at our network of clinics and wellness centers across India. Virtual consultations for some services are also available for convenience.

Tele-consultation:

Remote consultations with our wellness experts are available for ongoing guidance, follow-up care, and health support from the comfort of your home.

Who should use Wellness Testing & Support?

Individuals living with HIV, those at risk of HIV, and anyone who wants to monitor and improve their overall health, including physical and mental wellness.

When to use this service?

It is recommended to use this service regularly for general health maintenance, or when you need a comprehensive health assessment and guidance on lifestyle changes.

How to access Wellness Testing & Support?

Schedule an appointment online, either for in-clinic testing or remote consultation. We offer flexible options based on your location and availability.
