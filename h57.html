Fourth-Generation Testing

₹ 1799 ₹ 3500
Window Period: 2-4 Weeks

Fourth-Generation Testing is an advanced HIV diagnostic tool that combines the detection of both HIV antibodies and the p24 antigen, offering a more comprehensive and accurate screening compared to traditional tests. This innovative approach allows for earlier detection of HIV, typically within 2-4 weeks of exposure, by identifying the p24 antigen that appears before antibodies are detectable in the blood.

  • Comprehensive Detection: Detects both HIV-1 & HIV-2 antibodies and the p24 antigen, providing a broader and more reliable result compared to previous generations of testing.

-*Note:-
* A follow-up test may be recommended at 12 weeks to confirm results and ensure full accuracy.




Service Details



Purpose:

Fourth-Generation Testing is a highly advanced diagnostic method used to detect HIV infections. Unlike earlier tests, which only detect antibodies, fourth-generation tests identify both HIV antibodies and the p24 antigen, which can be detected as early as 2 to 3 weeks after exposure. This makes the test highly sensitive and allows for earlier and more accurate detection of HIV.

Services Offered:

Comprehensive HIV testing using the latest fourth-generation technology, providing early detection of HIV by identifying both HIV-1 and HIV-2 antibodies, along with the p24 antigen. This test reduces the waiting period for reliable results and offers greater accuracy than previous testing methods.

Special Preparation:

No special preparation is required for the Fourth-Generation Test. However, it’s recommended to wait at least 2 to 3 weeks after potential exposure to HIV for the most accurate results.

Cost:

The cost of the Fourth-Generation Testing is affordable, with options available for both individuals and groups. You can also check for insurance coverage or inquire about financial assistance if needed.

Location:

Available at diagnostic centers and partner clinics across the country. Home sample collection services are also available for your convenience.

Tele-consultation:

After your test, you can consult with one of our medical experts via telemedicine to discuss your results and determine the next steps for your health and treatment.

Who should take the Fourth-Generation Test?

The Fourth-Generation HIV Test is ideal for individuals who believe they may have been exposed to HIV, especially those at higher risk. It is recommended for those who require accurate results in the early stages after exposure, as it detects HIV earlier than antibody-only tests.

When to test?

You should wait at least 2 to 3 weeks from the time of exposure (e.g., unprotected sex, needle sharing) before taking the Fourth-Generation test. Testing too early may lead to inaccurate results due to the time it takes for antibodies and antigens to develop.

How to access the Fourth-Generation Test?

To take the Fourth-Generation test, you can book an appointment online or visit one of our diagnostic centers. We offer both home sample collection and in-clinic options for your convenience.
