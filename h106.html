Homeless Shelter HIV Services

₹ 1499 ₹ 3000
Window Period: 2-4 weeks

Homeless Shelter HIV Services offers confidential and compassionate HIV testing for individuals in homeless shelters. This service is designed to provide accessible healthcare to those in need, ensuring they receive accurate HIV testing and support in a safe, understanding, and non-judgmental environment.

With a window period of 2-4 weeks, Homeless Shelter HIV Services are ideal for early detection. The process is straightforward, offering quick and accurate results, while maintaining respect for the privacy and dignity of individuals seeking care.

-*Note:-
* A confirmatory test at a healthcare facility may be required if the result is positive.




Service Details



Window period:

2-6 weeks

Sample required:

Blood sample (fingerstick or venous)

Special Preparation:

No fasting or special preparation required

Reports:

Receive confidential results via e-report within 48 hours after testing.

Home sample collection:

Free home collection of samples for your convenience and privacy.

Tele-consult with Doctor:

Access a detailed consultation with an expert, at no extra charge, to discuss your results.

What does it test for?

HIV-1 and HIV-2 Antibodies
The At-Home Results service tests for both HIV-1 and HIV-2 antibodies, providing a discreet and accurate screening option.

Where should I get tested?

You can conveniently perform the test at home using the At-Home Results kit, designed for ease of use and privacy.

When to test?

It is recommended to wait 2-6 weeks after potential exposure for accurate results. For those who are sexually active or at high risk, regular testing is recommended.
