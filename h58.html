Service Details



Purpose:

Phenotype Profiling is an advanced test that evaluates how the HIV virus reacts to different antiretroviral drugs (ARVs). This test provides critical information about which drugs are most effective in treating your specific strain of HIV. By assessing the virus's response to various medications, phenotype profiling helps tailor a personalized treatment plan to improve treatment outcomes and avoid unnecessary drug resistance.

Services Offered:

Testing for HIV drug resistance by analyzing the virus's phenotype. This service helps doctors determine the best possible ARV regimen by identifying which drugs will most effectively suppress your specific HIV strain and prevent the development of resistance.

Special Preparation:

No special preparation is required for Phenotype Profiling. A blood sample is collected, and the test is typically recommended when patients show treatment failure or need guidance on selecting an effective antiretroviral regimen.

Cost:

The cost of Phenotype Profiling varies depending on the number of drugs tested and the complexity of the analysis. You can inquire about pricing or check for insurance coverage to ensure your testing is affordable.

Location:

Phenotype Profiling is available at specialized diagnostic centers and HIV treatment clinics. Home sample collection is also available for your convenience.

Tele-consultation:

After receiving your phenotype profiling results, you can schedule a tele-consultation with one of our HIV specialists to discuss your treatment options and make informed decisions about your care plan.

Who should undergo Phenotype Profiling?

Phenotype Profiling is highly recommended for individuals with HIV who are experiencing treatment failure or those who have developed resistance to their current antiretroviral regimen. It is also useful for individuals starting a new treatment to ensure the best possible therapeutic outcome.

When to test?

Phenotype Profiling is typically performed when there are signs of treatment failure, such as rising viral load or insufficient response to antiretroviral therapy. It can also be done before starting a new treatment regimen to optimize drug selection.

How to access Phenotype Profiling?

You can schedule a Phenotype Profiling test through your healthcare provider or by booking directly with our diagnostic center. We offer home sample collection for your convenience and ensure that you receive personalized care throughout the process.
