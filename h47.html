Community HIV Support

Free
Window Period: N/A

Community HIV Support is a vital service that focuses on providing ongoing support, education, and resources for individuals living with HIV, as well as those at risk. This service fosters a sense of community, reduces stigma, and helps individuals lead healthy, fulfilling lives. Whether you're newly diagnosed or have been living with HIV for years, our community support offers resources to assist you. The service includes:

  • Peer Support Groups: Connect with others living with HIV in a safe, confidential space to share experiences, offer support, and build a network of understanding and solidarity.

-*Note:-
* All services are free, confidential, and available to individuals living with HIV and their families or partners.




Service Details



Purpose:

Providing comprehensive support to individuals and communities affected by HIV, focusing on education, prevention, stigma reduction, and access to treatment.

Services Offered:

Community outreach, educational workshops, HIV testing campaigns, support groups, and assistance with accessing HIV-related healthcare and social services.

Special Preparation:

No special preparation is needed. We encourage open communication, and participation in community activities is welcome.

Cost:

Most community outreach services are free of charge. Some specialized services may have a minimal fee or be covered by health programs.

Location:

Available at community centers and local clinics across India. We also offer online resources and virtual support groups.

Tele-consultation:

Community members can access online consultations and support group meetings to connect with experts and other individuals living with HIV.

Who should participate in Community HIV Support?

Individuals living with HIV, their families, caregivers, and anyone seeking more information and support regarding HIV prevention, treatment, and mental health.

When to participate?

Whenever you need support, information, or want to engage in a community of individuals affected by HIV. Participation is encouraged regularly or as needed for special events or workshops.

How to get involved?

You can join our community outreach programs, attend workshops and awareness events, or join online support groups through our website or local centers. Volunteers are always welcome to help with outreach efforts.
