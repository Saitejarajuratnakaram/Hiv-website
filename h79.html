Early Stage Detection - Timely HIV Diagnosis

₹ 499 ₹ 1500
Accurate Results in 2-3 Weeks

Early Stage Detection is designed to identify HIV during its initial phases, enabling prompt care and better health outcomes. With advanced testing methods, this service provides fast, reliable results in a confidential and supportive environment.

Taking the first step toward early detection ensures timely treatment, reduces transmission risk, and supports long-term well-being.

-*Note:-
* Early diagnosis plays a critical role in managing and preventing HIV progression.




Service Details



Window Period:

Detect HIV as early as 2-3 weeks after potential exposure using advanced screening techniques.

Sample Required:

A minimal blood sample is used for precise early-stage detection.

Special Preparation:

No fasting or special preparation is needed before the test.

Reports:

Get your results on-site instantly or securely delivered via email.

Home Sample Collection:

Convenient home collection services are available upon request.

Tele-consult with Doctor:

Receive expert advice on your results and next steps through online consultations.

What Does the Test Include?

- HIV p24 antigen for early detection
- HIV antibodies for HIV-1 and HIV-2
- Comprehensive early-stage health assessment

Where Can I Get Tested?

Visit one of our nationwide centers or opt for a home sample collection at your convenience.

When Should I Test?

Testing is recommended 2-3 weeks after potential exposure or for individuals seeking early detection to ensure timely care.
